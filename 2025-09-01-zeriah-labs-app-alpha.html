Primary 1–2 Chinese app enters alpha

Sep 1, 2025 · Zeriah Labs

Zeriah Labs begins early testing for its Primary Chinese learning app with a small group of families and educators.

We’re focusing on short, playful sessions designed for Primary 1–2 learners, with clear goals and gentle scaffolding. Early feedback will inform lesson flows, accessibility tweaks, and content roadmap.

Families interested in participating can register interest via our Contact page. We’ll expand access gradually as we validate each module.
