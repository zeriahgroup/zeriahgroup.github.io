Aug 12, 2025 · Zeriah Group

Brand system update for Group, Labs, Studios

We refreshed our wordmarks and unified the design language across the Zeriah portfolio, improving accessibility, clarity, and cohesion across Group, Labs, and Studios. This update reflects our goal of presenting a consistent, family-friendly identity to our communities worldwide.

The changes include refined typography, simplified logo usage guidelines, and clearer separation between the three brands. Accessibility contrast and responsive scaling were also prioritized, so the marks remain legible across devices and environments.
